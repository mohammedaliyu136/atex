About ShopWave

We're on a mission to make online shopping accessible, enjoyable, and trustworthy for everyone.

Our Story

ShopWave was founded in 2024 with a simple idea: create an e-commerce platform that puts people first. What started as a small marketplace has grown into a vibrant community of thousands of buyers and sellers.

Today, we serve over 28,000 customers across multiple categories including electronics, fashion, home goods, and sports equipment. Our platform processes thousands of orders monthly with a 99.5% satisfaction rate.
